ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Nora Liu!

Welcome to my creative space on the internet! I am currently a second-year computer engineering student at the University of Toronto. Outside of being a student, I also enjoy visual arts and photography. This website is a space where I bring together my technical expertise and creative pursuits.

My Journey into Visual Arts and Engineering

My journey into the world of art began as a hobby, leading me to audition and attend an arts high school! There, I graduated with a regional visual arts certificate, laying a strong foundation for my creative endeavours. While those four years of high school were filled with creative exploration, I also discovered a strong interest in math and sciences, which ultimately guided me toward engineering. Now that I'm in computer engineering and love what I'm studying, I still enjoy revisiting my creative pursuits during my spare time with visual arts!

Visual Arts

My favourite medium is oil painting, and you'll see a lot of that on this website. I also love using gouache, watercolour, and graphite. A lot of my artwork reflects what I worked on during high school, showcasing my creative growth and exploration. You can find more about my art here.

Why This Website?

I created this website as a personal portfolio to showcase my art and photography. It's a place where I can share my artistic projects, document my creative process, and connect with others who share similar interests.

Let's Connect!

Feel free to explore my website and reach out if you have any questions or just want to say hello! I'm always open to collaboration and new opportunities.
